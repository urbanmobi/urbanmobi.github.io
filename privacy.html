Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 1, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pothole Hunter ("we," "us," or "our"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the Pothole Hunter application. By using our app, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Location Data: We collect your location data only when the app is running and with your explicit permission. We do not continuously track your location or collect real-time location data. Location data is used solely for the purpose of pinpointing pothole locations when you choose to report them.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about how you interact with the app, such as your device type, operating system, and app usage patterns. This data is collected anonymously and used to enhance the app's performance and user experience.

3. Data Storage and Analytics

We use Google Firebase for data storage and analytics. Your data is securely stored and analyzed by Firebase services. For more information on how Google handles your data, please review Google's Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends.
  • To enhance the functionality and user experience of the app.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your location or personal information with third parties. Your data is used exclusively for app-related purposes.

6. Your Choices

You have the right to control your location data permissions within the app. You can choose to provide or revoke location access at any time.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our data practices. You are encouraged to review this policy periodically for updates.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at urbanmobi.helpdesk@gmail.com.
